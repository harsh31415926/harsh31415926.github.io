About

Systems that reason —
not just respond.

I'm an AI Engineer who builds agentic systems: applications where language models don't just answer questions, but orchestrate tools, hold memory, and carry out multi-step plans. My recent work centers on LangGraph and the Model Context Protocol — coordinating multiple agents that retrieve information, call external tools, and reason toward a goal together.

Alongside agentic AI, I work across the machine learning stack — building classification and regression models for problems like customer churn, pricing, and predictive analytics, and shipping them as usable products rather than notebooks.

I was a finalist at Smart India Hackathon with a student dropout predictor, an educational intelligence system aimed at identifying at-risk students before it's too late. I care about building AI that's useful in production, not just impressive in a demo.

Multi-Agent AI Chatbot
